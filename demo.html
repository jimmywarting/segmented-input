segmented-input demo

Click inside any field and use ← / → to navigate between segments, ↑ / ↓ to increment or decrement, and Backspace to clear a segment. Tab / Shift+Tab also cycle through segments. Green border = valid, red border = invalid/incomplete.

Segments: r · g · b · a — placeholder: rgba(r, g, b, a) Segments: octet 1 · octet 2 · octet 3 · octet 4 Segments: hours · minutes · seconds — placeholder: hh:mm:ss Segments: 6 × byte (hex) — 00 in any position is valid Segments: 8-4-4-4-12 hex groups
